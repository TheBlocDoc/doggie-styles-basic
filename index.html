about us

we do things differently

At XtremeDoggieStylz by Carmela, we're not just about grooming dogs; we're about transforming them into four-legged fashionistas! With more than 16 years of dedicated experience in mobile dog grooming, our passion is to pamper your furry friends and have them step out in style.

Our mission is simple: to make every dog look and feel like a superstar. Whether it's a chic trim, a lavish spa day, or a full-blown makeover, we've got tails wagging and tongues panting with excitement. Because when Fluff Meets Fashion, it's a whole new level of fabulous!

Join us on a journey where every pawprint leads to a runway, and where grooming isn't just a service; it's a statement. Welcome to the world of Doggy Stylez by Carmela, where your pup's charisma meets our scissors, and the result is sheer canine elegance

  • Nail Trim - Our groomers will expertly trim all nails, including dew claws, to alleviate discomfort or pain and prevent overgrowth.

  • Nail Trim and File - In addition to the traditional trim, our groomers use a Dremel tool to painlessly smooth and shorten nails, reducing damage to furniture and flooring.

  • Scoop Paw Pads - This service removes excess hair between paw pads, promoting better traction.

  • Restorative Paw Pad Treatment - Our revolutionary formula for dogs and cats strengthens and repairs dry damaged coats and heals cracked paw pads.

  • Ear Plucking - Excess ear hair removal helps prevent ear infections.

  • Ear Flush (Deep Ear Clean) - A thorough flush with a specialized solution to deodorize, clean, and dry ear canals, promoting healthy ears.

  • Face Trim - Quick trimming around the eyes and face for a well-groomed appearance.

  • Potty Patch/Sanitary Trim - A neat trim in sensitive areas keeps things clean and tidy during bathroom breaks

  • Anal Gland Expression: For pets with difficulty expressing their own anal glands, our grooming staff can help with external expressions.

  • Oral Care Packages: Teeth cleaning with enzymatic toothpaste for a healthier smile.

  • Brushout: Removal of excess shedding hair or detangling matted hair. Call ahead to schedule a brushout.
